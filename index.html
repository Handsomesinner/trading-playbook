Price Behavior Rules

Universal truths about how price moves. These are the foundation of every setup you will ever take.

01

Price Moves in Ranges

Before entering any trade, identify the current range. Mark the high and the low. Your entry exists within this context ‚Äî never trade blind without knowing the boundaries. The range IS your framework.

02

Liquidity Gets Cleared Before Expansion

Price sweeps liquidity ‚Äî stop losses, equal highs/lows ‚Äî before the real move begins. Wait for the sweep. Entering before the liquidity grab is how you become the liquidity.

03

Gaps Get Filled

Fair Value Gaps and imbalances act as magnets. Price tends to return to fill them before continuing. Use FVGs as targets or as confluence zones for entry. They're your roadmap.
